# Search Car Rental

## Metadata
- **url**: https://guidetoiceland.is
- **viewport**: desktop
- **tags**: search, cars, booking
- **priority**: high

## Steps
1. Navigate to the homepage
2. Go to the Cars Tab
3. Choose any random dates in the date range picker, few weeks in advance
3. Click on 'Search' button to see all car rentals

## Expected Outcome
Search results page loads with Car Rental Cards visible.

## Assertions
- [ ] Search results page is displayed
- [ ] At least one Car Rental Card is visible
